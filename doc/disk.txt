This is documentation relating to disk management.
enter paths to the disks as command line arguments
All olympus virtual floppy disks are read by the olympus 100 as either program disks, or data disks.
Program disks MUST be inserted into slot a.
You may copy a data disk by inserting the disk you wish to copy from into slot a and the disk you wish to copy to into slot b
Disks are inserted into slot b as read/write, and slot a as read only.
Disks are 128 Kibibytes in size.
Disks are divided into 16 sectors of 8 kibibytes.
Sectors of disk a are loaded 4 at a time beginning at RAM address $8000
Sectors of disk b are loaded 2 at a time beginning at RAM address $4000
